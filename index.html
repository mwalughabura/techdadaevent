About

RISPER AKINYI

She is one of the co-founders of ‘Tech Dada,’ which seeks to improve the gender imbalance in technology that is prevalent in many communities around informal region. She is a student as well as a volunteer at Learning Lions in Turkana. She is a Strong believer of women empowerment. She is the lead, facilitating Techdada in Turkana. She believes in creating a space where by both gender believe in themselves, become the best they can ever be, and feel they belong without discrimination and are appreciated in whatever they are passionate in.
